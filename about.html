The Course

Throughout the semester, Mr. Kerney's CSCI 45 students were tasked with creating projects which highlighted the versatility and capabilities of the pocket-sized computer - the Raspberry Pi.

Groups and Materials

The class was divided into groups of 3-4. At the start of the semester, every student recieved two Pis, an LCD screen, and two sensor kits. Additional materials were purchased at the teams own expense.
